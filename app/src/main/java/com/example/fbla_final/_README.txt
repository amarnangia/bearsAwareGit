The Bears Aware App
This app is for bridgeland high school students, parents, staff, and the local community.
It provides you a way to easily access resources and information for the school

Getting Started

Purpose
The purpose of this app is to pool difference resources that students, parents, and staff need in one place for them to easily access.
Our app also wants to help unite our community through raising awareness for school events, as well as allowing students and staff to
share images in a local, school-wide social media way.

Prerequisites
Android phones but be Android 11+ as of right now

Installation
In order to get this on your device or use the app:
    Upload the app onto either a plugged in device, or an emulator
    To connect a physical device,
        since the app is not published yet, plug it into this device and follow instruction in device manager
    To create an emulator,
        open device manager in your IDE and follow instructions
**in the future, the app will be published on teh app store and google play store for users to download from

Usage
To start the app, once it is downloaded, the user just needs to click on the app icon as they would any other app
The app will start itself and tell you what to do next. Navigation is straightforward and easy to understand. All
options are labeled with exactly how to use them
